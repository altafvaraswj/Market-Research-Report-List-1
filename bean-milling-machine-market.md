<p><p>The bean milling machine market can be categorized into vertical and horizontal machines. Vertical machines utilize a vertically oriented milling process, offering efficient grinding for various types of beans, and are often preferred for their compact design and ease of maintenance. In contrast, horizontal machines feature a horizontal orientation, providing larger capacity and better uniformity in particle size. Both types cater to different industrial needs, with vertical machines focusing on smaller operations and horizontal machines aimed at high-volume production.</p></p>
