<p><p>The injection molding machine servo system market is divided into two primary types: low power servo systems and high power servo systems. Low power servo systems are typically used for small to medium-sized injection molding machines, offering energy efficiency and precise control for less demanding applications. In contrast, high power servo systems are designed for larger machines requiring significant force and speed, catering to high-volume production and complex molding processes. Both types enhance performance and reduce energy consumption, contributing to operational cost savings.</p></p>
