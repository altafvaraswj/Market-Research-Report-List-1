<p><p>Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) technology is a form of 3D printing that utilizes a laser to fuse powdered materials, typically plastics, metals, or ceramics, into a solid structure. The process involves layer-by-layer printing, where a laser selectively sinters the powder to create intricate geometries that are difficult or impossible to achieve with traditional manufacturing methods. SLS is favored for its ability to produce functional prototypes and end-use parts with high strength, durability, and design flexibility.</p><p>The Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) Technology for 3D Printing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.6% during the forecast period.
