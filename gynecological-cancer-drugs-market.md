<p><p>The gynecological cancer drugs market comprises three main types: chemotherapy, targeted therapy, and hormonal therapy. Chemotherapy involves using powerful medications to kill rapidly dividing cancer cells and can affect healthy cells as well. Targeted therapy focuses on specific molecular targets associated with cancer, aiming to minimize damage to normal cells while inhibiting cancer growth. Hormonal therapy, on the other hand, addresses cancers influenced by hormones by blocking or altering hormone activity, thereby slowing down or stopping cancer progression.</p></p>
