<p><strong>The Modelling Clay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Dry Clay</li><li>Air-Dry Clay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modeling clay market is primarily divided into two types: non-dry clay and air-dry clay. Non-dry clay, often oil-based or polymer clay, maintains its malleability until cured through baking, making it ideal for intricate designs and detailed projects. Conversely, air-dry clay hardens upon exposure to air, eliminating the need for baking, which appeals to casual crafters and children. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with non-dry clay favored for professional use and air-dry clay preferred for ease and convenience.</p></p>
