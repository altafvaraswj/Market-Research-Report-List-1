<p><p>The Leakage ELISA Kit market comprises four main types: Direct ELISA, which detects antigens directly using labeled antibodies; Sandwich ELISA, which captures the antigen between two antibodies for enhanced sensitivity; Indirect ELISA, where an unlabeled primary antibody binds the antigen and a labeled secondary antibody detects it; and Competitive ELISA, which measures the concentration of antigen by competing with a labeled form. Each type serves specific research and diagnostic purposes, influencing market trends and applications in various fields.</p></p>
