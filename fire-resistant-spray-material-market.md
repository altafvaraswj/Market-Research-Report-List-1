<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire-resistant spray material market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based sprays. Water-based fire-resistant materials are eco-friendly, easy to clean, and have lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them safer for indoor applications. Solvent-based sprays, on the other hand, typically offer superior adhesion and durability, making them suitable for harsh environments. Both types serve to enhance fire safety in various structures, offering protection to materials by forming an insulating layer when exposed to high temperatures.</p></p>
