<p><ul><li>Static Retorts</li><li>Rotary Retorts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sterilization retorts market features two primary types: static retorts and rotary retorts. Static retorts utilize a stationary chamber where products are placed on racks and sterilized using steam or hot water, ensuring even heat distribution. In contrast, rotary retorts employ a rotating mechanism to continuously move products through the sterilization process, improving heat transfer and reducing processing times. Both types are essential for ensuring food safety and shelf stability in canned, jarred, and packaged food products.</p></p>
