<p><p>Solid Phase Extraction (SPE) is a sample preparation technique used to separate and concentrate analytes from complex mixtures. The market is categorized primarily into Normal Phase SPE and Reversed Phase SPE. Normal Phase SPE utilizes a polar stationary phase and is effective for polar compounds, while Reversed Phase SPE uses a nonpolar stationary phase, making it suitable for nonpolar analytes. Both methods are widely employed in environmental, pharmaceutical, and clinical laboratories for efficient sample purification and analysis.</p></p>
