<p><strong>The Private Mortgage Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Borrower-paid (BPMI)</li><li>Lender-paid (LPMI)</li><li>Single Premium</li><li>Split Premium</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Private Mortgage Insurance (PMI) is essential for borrowers with small down payments, protecting lenders against default. There are several market types: **Borrower-Paid PMI (BPMI)** involves monthly premiums paid by the borrower until the loan-to-value ratio is acceptable. **Lender-Paid PMI (LPMI)** allows the lender to cover the insurance cost, typically with a higher interest rate. **Single Premium PMI** requires a one-time upfront payment at closing, while **Split Premium PMI** combines both upfront and monthly payments for flexibility. Each type balances cost and risk.</p></p>
