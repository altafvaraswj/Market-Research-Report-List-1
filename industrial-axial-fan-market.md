<p><p>The industrial axial fan market is primarily divided into two types: plate type and tube type. Plate type fans feature a flat design, optimizing airflow for larger areas and often used in HVAC systems. Conversely, tube type fans have a cylindrical shape that enhances airflow direction and is ideal for confined spaces or where installation flexibility is required. Both types cater to various industrial applications, ensuring efficient ventilation and cooling by effectively moving air across different environments.</p></p>
