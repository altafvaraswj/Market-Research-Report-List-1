<p><p>The 3D LiDAR sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Solid State LiDAR and Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR. Solid State LiDAR utilizes a fixed sensor design with no moving parts, resulting in enhanced durability and compact size, ideal for automotive and robotics applications. In contrast, Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR employs rotating components to capture data over a wide field of view, making it suitable for applications like mapping and surveying. Both technologies play crucial roles in diverse sectors, including autonomous vehicles and infrastructure monitoring.</p></p>
