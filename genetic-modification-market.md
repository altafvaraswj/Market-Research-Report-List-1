<p><p>The genetic modification market encompasses three main types: molecular cloning, gene delivery, and genome editing. Molecular cloning involves isolating and replicating DNA fragments to study gene functions. Gene delivery focuses on transporting genetic material into cells for therapeutic purposes, often utilizing vectors or nanoparticles. Genome editing allows precise alterations to an organism's DNA, with tools like CRISPR-Cas9 enabling targeted modifications. Together, these methods advance biotechnology, agriculture, and medicine by enhancing genetic traits and developing new therapies.</p></p>
