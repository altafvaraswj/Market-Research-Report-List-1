<p><strong>The RGB Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radio Remote Control</li><li>Infrared Remote Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RGB controller market comprises various types, primarily including Radio Remote Control and Infrared Remote Control systems. Radio remote controls utilize radio frequency signals to operate RGB lighting from a distance, providing a wider range and unobstructed functionality. In contrast, infrared remote controls require a direct line of sight to transmit signals to the RGB devices. Each type offers distinct advantages, with radio options facilitating more flexibility and infrared systems being cost-effective for shorter-range applications.</p></p>
