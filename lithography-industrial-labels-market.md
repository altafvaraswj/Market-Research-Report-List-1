<p><p>The lithography industrial labels market can be categorized into two primary types: metal and polymer. Metal labels, often made from materials like aluminum and brass, are favored for their durability and resistance to harsh environments, making them ideal for industrial applications. Conversely, polymer labels, typically produced from materials like vinyl or polyethylene, offer flexibility and are lightweight. They are more cost-effective and are frequently used for short-term applications and less demanding conditions. Both types serve crucial roles in branding, identification, and compliance across various industries.</p></p>
