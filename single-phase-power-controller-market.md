<p><ul><li>Resistive Power Controller</li><li>Nonresistive Power Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Single-phase Power Controller market comprises two main types: Resistive Power Controllers and Nonresistive Power Controllers. Resistive Power Controllers manage electrical power in resistive loads, such as heaters and incandescent lights, providing precise control over temperature and brightness. In contrast, Nonresistive Power Controllers are designed for reactive loads, like motors and transformers, managing power factor and improving energy efficiency. Both types serve crucial roles in various applications, including industrial, commercial, and residential settings, ensuring optimal energy management and performance.</p></p>
