<p><ul><li>Pelvis</li><li>Whole Body</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The birth simulator market is divided into two main types: pelvis simulators and whole body simulators. Pelvis simulators focus on realistic birthing scenarios by replicating the female pelvic anatomy, allowing medical professionals to practice various delivery techniques. Whole body simulators provide a comprehensive training experience, integrating both the mother's and baby's anatomical features, enabling practitioners to simulate complete childbirth processes, including prenatal and postnatal care. Both types enhance training efficacy in obstetrics and gynecology.</p></p>
