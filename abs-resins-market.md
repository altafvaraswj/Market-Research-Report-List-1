<p><p>The ABS resins market is divided into two main types: General Purpose ABS and Specialty ABS. General Purpose ABS is widely used for its balance of strength, toughness, and impact resistance, making it ideal for consumer products and automotive applications. Specialty ABS, on the other hand, is tailored for specific applications requiring enhanced characteristics, such as higher thermal stability, UV resistance, or improved chemical resistance.
