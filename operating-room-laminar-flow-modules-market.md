<p><p>Operating Room Laminar Flow Modules are critical for maintaining infection control and optimal air quality in surgical environments. The market is segmented into two types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal laminar flow modules direct airflow in a parallel manner across the surgical site, offering broad coverage and minimal turbulence. In contrast, vertical modules deliver airflow from above, creating a sterile zone while minimizing the risk of contamination from personnel movements. Both types aim to enhance surgical safety and patient outcomes by ensuring a clean operating environment.</p></p>
