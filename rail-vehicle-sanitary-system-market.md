<p><strong>The Rail Vehicle Sanitary System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Toilet System</li><li>Pressured Water Flushing Toilet System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rail vehicle sanitary system market comprises two primary types: vacuum toilet systems and pressured water flushing toilet systems. Vacuum toilet systems operate by creating a pressure differential, efficiently removing waste with minimal water, making them ideal for long-distance trains. In contrast, pressured water flushing toilet systems utilize pressurized water to flush waste away, offering a more conventional approach. Both systems serve to maintain hygiene and comfort for passengers, adapting to varying operational needs and environmental conditions in the rail industry.</p></p>
