<p><p>The austenitic stainless steel market is primarily segmented into types 304, 316, 309, and 310. Type 304 is widely used for its corrosion resistance and weldability, making it ideal for food processing. Type 316 offers enhanced resistance to aggressive environments, particularly in chemical and marine applications. Type 309 provides high-temperature strength and oxidation resistance, suitable for industrial furnace applications. Type 310, known for its superior heat resistance, is often utilized in high-temperature applications such as heat exchangers and kiln linings.</p></p>
