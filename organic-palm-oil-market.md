<p><ul><li>Crude Palm Oil</li><li>Palm Olein</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic palm oil market primarily consists of two types: Crude Palm Oil (CPO) and Palm Olein. CPO is the unrefined oil extracted from the fruit of the oil palm, known for its high carotenoid content and various applications in food and cosmetics. Palm Olein, the liquid fraction obtained from the fractionation of CPO, is favored for cooking due to its stability at high temperatures and light color. Both types cater to the growing demand for organic and sustainable products.</p></p>
