<p><p>The orthopedic regenerative surgical market encompasses various treatments aimed at enhancing tissue repair and regeneration. Allografts involve using donor tissues to replace damaged structures, promoting healing. Cell-based treatments utilize stem cells or progenitor cells to stimulate regeneration at the injury site. Viscosupplements involve injecting hyaluronic acid to improve joint lubrication and reduce pain, particularly in osteoarthritis. Synthetic treatments use engineered materials to mimic biological properties, providing support and enhancing recovery in orthopedic applications. Together, these approaches enhance patient outcomes and recovery.</p></p>
