<p><p>The drug detector market encompasses various technologies for identifying illicit substances. X-ray drug detectors utilize radiation to visualize concealed drugs in baggage or on individuals. MRI drug detectors leverage magnetic resonance imaging for detailed internal scans, aiding in the detection of substances within the body. Steam drug detectors employ vapor to analyze exhaled breath for drug traces, while neutron drug detectors use neutron radiography to identify materials through their atomic composition. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing security and law enforcement efforts.</p></p>
