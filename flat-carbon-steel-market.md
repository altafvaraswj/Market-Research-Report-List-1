<p><p>The flat carbon steel market is categorized into three main types based on carbon content: low carbon, medium carbon, and high carbon. Low carbon steel, containing up to 0.3% carbon, is malleable and ductile, making it ideal for automotive and construction applications. Medium carbon steel, with 0.3% to 0.6% carbon, offers a balance of strength and toughness, suitable for machinery and structural components. High carbon steel, containing 0.6% to 1.4% carbon, is known for its hardness and tensile strength, often used in cutting tools and high-wear applications.</p></p>
