<p><p>The silver-based antimicrobials market is categorized by various carrier types that influence their application and effectiveness. Silicate carriers enhance stability and release of silver ions in various environments. Phosphate carriers offer targeted delivery and sustained antimicrobial action, ideal for medical and agricultural uses. Titanium dioxide carriers combine photocatalytic properties with silver's antimicrobial effects, beneficial in water treatment and self-cleaning surfaces. Glass carriers provide structural integrity and controlled silver release, making them suitable for coatings and biomedical devices.</p></p>
