<p><p>The dry heat disinfect sterilizer market is characterized by two main types: Thermal Radiation Dry Heat Sterilizers and Thermal Laminar Flow Heating Dry Heat Sterilizers. Thermal Radiation Dry Heat Sterilizers utilize high temperatures to achieve sterilization through radiant heat, effectively eliminating microorganisms on various surfaces. In contrast, Thermal Laminar Flow Heating Dry Heat Sterilizers combine direct heat with laminar airflow, ensuring uniform temperature distribution and minimizing contamination risks, making them suitable for critical applications in healthcare and laboratories.</p></p>
