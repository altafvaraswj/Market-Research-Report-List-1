<p><p>Belt markets are categorized based on their design and application. Flat belts are used for general power transmission. Round belts are flexible and suited for light-duty applications. V belts provide a wedged surface for efficient grip in machines. Multi-groove belts are designed for compactness and high power transfer across multiple pulleys. Timing belts have teeth for precise movement synchronization. Ribbed belts enhance flexibility and grip. Other types include specialty belts for unique industrial needs, ensuring a broad range of applications across various sectors.</p></p>
