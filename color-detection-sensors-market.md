<p><p>The color detection sensors market consists of various types, each serving distinct functions. Color sensors measure specific color shades, allowing for precise identification and sorting. Brightness sensors evaluate the intensity of light, impacting applications such as display technology. Contrast sensors assess the difference between light and dark areas, crucial for quality control and image processing in various industries.</p></p>
