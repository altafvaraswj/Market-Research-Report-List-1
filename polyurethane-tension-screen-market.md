<p><strong>The Polyurethane Tension Screen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transversal Tension</li><li>Longitudinal Tension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane tension screen market is categorized primarily into two types: transversal tension and longitudinal tension. Transversal tension screens feature strings aligned horizontally, providing enhanced filtration efficiency for materials moving across the surface. In contrast, longitudinal tension screens have strings arranged vertically, which are designed for more effective drainage and material separation along the length of the screen. Both types cater to various industrial applications, including mining, construction, and recycling, optimizing performance based on specific separation requirements.</p></p>
